PUBG Mobile Prestige: A Guide to the 10 Most Sought-After Titles

Elevate your PUBG Mobile game by mastering the 10 exclusive titles that highlight your skills and dominance—check out the requirements and strategies!

Published on: October 18, 2024 | By Deepti Ratnam

In PUBG Mobile, players can earn various titles by achieving specific goals that reflect their skills and accomplishments in the game. Here are the 10 titles in PUBG Mobile and how to obtain them:

Commando:

To obtain the Commando title, players need to win 50 solo classic matches without using any armor or helmets. This challenging feat tests pure combat skills and survival instinct, as players must rely entirely on their offensive capabilities and game sense without the protection of armor.

Fishing Expert (or Leader of Gains):

This unofficial title refers to a player’s ability to loot and manage resources effectively. While there’s no direct title for this, mastering looting and gaining the upper hand through good resource management is key to long-term survival in PUBG Mobile

Skilled Sniper:

This title is for players who excel at long-range combat. To earn it, players need to secure multiple headshots or kills from a distance using sniper rifles, showcasing precise aim and timing.

Destiny:

The title of destiny could represent earning wins in an unconventional way, such as through luck-based kills like grenades or tricky circle situations. It highlights moments where fortune plays a major role in achieving victory.

Collector:

This title is earned by gathering a wide range of PUBG Mobile items, such as skins, clothing, and accessories. Players with this title show off their diverse and comprehensive in-game collection.

Reaching the Limit:

This title could signify players who consistently push their game to the highest ranks or those who achieve maximum stats across kills, wins, and survival.

Weapon Expert:

To achieve this title, players must secure kills using a variety of weapons in a single classic match, proving mastery over multiple weapon types.

Excess Conductor:

This title could refer to leading multiple squads to victory and showing strong leadership and coordination in team games.

Peaceful One (or Free Chicken Dinner):

The Peaceful One title is earned by winning a match without killing any enemies. It rewards strategic movement, survival, and intelligent gameplay over combat.

Well Liked:

The Well-Liked title in PUBG Mobile is a recognition of a player’s social popularity within the game. To earn this title, players need to receive a total of 1,000 likes from other players.
